Why T2R exists

Theory to Reality was created to help engineering students connect academic learning with real-world project experience, professional tools, and practical career direction.

T2R brings industry experts, project examples, and global perspectives into student learning so they can make better choices before graduation.

It is designed for institutions, students, and young professionals who want learning to feel more practical, relevant, and connected to real careers.
